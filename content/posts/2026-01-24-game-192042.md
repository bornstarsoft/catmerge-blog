On January 21, gamers can dive into the vibrant world of Exotica 2: Pet Shop Simulator, now available on PC. This unique simulator not only focuses on the joy of caring for exotic animals but also challenges players to manage a thriving pet shop. With over 70 diverse animal species to interact with, it promises a rich and engaging experience for animal lovers and simulation fans alike.

The game is set to expand its reach beyond PC, with plans for releases on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and Nintendo Switch 2. While the exact dates for these console launches remain under wraps, the anticipation is building. Will Exotica 2 redefine the pet simulation genre and attract a new wave of players?
