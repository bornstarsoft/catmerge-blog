Hungry Studio's flagship title, Block Blast!, has been crowned the "Best Puzzle Game" at the Sensor Tower APAC Awards 2025, a testament to its widespread popularity and innovative gameplay. This accolade is based on critical metrics such as download velocity and user retention, highlighting how players around the globe have embraced the game. With its unique drag-match-disappear mechanic and availability in over 200 countries, Block Blast! has successfully ranked No. 1 in more than 40 markets.

In 2025, Hungry Studio enhanced its presence in the APAC region through localized strategies, including prefecture-based high-score challenges in Japan and celebrity collaborations in Korea. These efforts not only solidified Block Blast! as a global hit but also transformed it into a beloved local favorite, seamlessly integrating into the daily lives of diverse players.

As Jason Wang, VP of Brand Marketing at Hungry Studio, expressed, this recognition motivates the team to continue pushing boundaries and innovating in the gaming space. With multiple awards under its belt this year, including the App Ape Award and the Asia-Pacific Stevie Awards, Hungry Studio is poised to evolve alongside its player community.
