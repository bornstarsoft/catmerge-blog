His plans for the winnings are simple yet wise; he intends to save the money. Balancing his work as an HVAC technician with a side gig in hair cutting, he exemplifies how unexpected financial windfalls can be approached with prudence. The 7-Eleven store that sold the winning ticket also benefits, receiving a $500 bonus for the sale.
