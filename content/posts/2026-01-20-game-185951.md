If you thrive on creating order and aesthetics in your gaming experience, then the upcoming game Hozy is bound to capture your attention. This delightful sandbox puzzle game combines elements of cleaning and decorating, reminiscent of beloved titles like Unpacking and PowerWash Simulator. With its engaging mechanics, Hozy offers players the chance to tidy up and personalize spaces in a way that feels both satisfying and creatively freeing.

In Hozy, players will find themselves immersed in various levels where they can clean and decorate different rooms. The gameplay allows for a unique blend of tidying up and artistic expression, as you can arrange furniture and decor items to your liking.
