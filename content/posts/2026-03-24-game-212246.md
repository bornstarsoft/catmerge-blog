The excitement is building as SEGAs latest project, Stranger Than Heaven, is set to take center stage in the upcoming Xbox Partner Preview event on March 26. This digital showcase will provide an in-depth look at the game, which is shrouded in mystery but promises to deliver a unique experience set in Japan in 1915. Fans of the Like a Dragon/Yakuza series are particularly eager to see how this new title fits into the broader gaming landscape.

Scheduled for broadcast at 10 AM Pacific / 1 PM Eastern / 5 PM UK, the event will also feature updates on other highly anticipated titles like S.T.A.L.K.E.R. 2: Heart of Chornobyl and The Expanse: Osiris Reborn. With world premieres and Xbox Game Pass announcements on the agenda, this event is a must-watch for gamers and industry enthusiasts alike.

As we gear up for the event, one can't help but wonder: will Stranger Than Heaven redefine our expectations for narrative-driven games? Stay tuned for what could be a groundbreaking moment in gaming history.
