Get ready for an exhilarating experience as the urban survival game 'Good Luck' prepares to launch on PC on April 8. Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer challenges players to navigate a city filled with absurd traps and hazards. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, it promises to deliver a unique blend of fun and chaos.

As players race to reach a meeting point, they'll encounter a variety of unusual challenges that test their skills and teamwork. The game is set to expand its reach to PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S consoles later on, making it accessible to a wider audience. With its quirky design and engaging gameplay, 'Good Luck' is poised to become a favorite among co-op gaming enthusiasts.

Are you ready to tackle the unpredictable world of 'Good Luck' and see if you can survive the urban jungle? Keep an eye out for its release and prepare for some unforgettable gaming moments!
