The upcoming release of "Save My Scrap" is set to redefine the visual novel and puzzle game landscape. Launching on April 22, 2026, on Steam, followed by console releases on April 30 for Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 5, and May 28 for Xbox Series X|S, this game promises an immersive experience that blends emotional storytelling with intricate gameplay mechanics. 

Originally a free game titled "Fix My Junk," this upgraded version features new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode, all crafted by the original developer, Unio Anou. Players will not only engage in mechanical repairs of a broken android but also navigate a narrative that delves into themes of memory, connection, and emotional depth. With multilingual support and a comfortable play experience, it caters to a global audience eager for rich storytelling.

As we approach the launch, the excitement builds around how "Save My Scrap" will resonate with players and redefine their expectations of the genre. Will this game set a new standard for emotional engagement in interactive storytelling? Let's find out together!
