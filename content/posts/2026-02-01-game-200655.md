As gamers, we often seek out cozy experiences that provide comfort and relaxation. However, some games on Steam take this concept and turn it on its head, challenging players to think differently. These surprising cozy games invite you to reshape your environment, solve intricate puzzles, and view the world through a new lens.

One standout title is **Viewfinder**, an adventure puzzle game where taking a picture allows you to manipulate the landscape around you. This innovative mechanic encourages exploration and creativity, making every puzzle feel like a unique journey. Another gem, **Gorogoa**, transforms the way you interact with art through its panel-based puzzles that require you to rearrange beautifully hand-drawn illustrations to progress. Lastly, **Superliminal** offers a dream-like experience where perspective is key, blending reality-bending puzzles with a touch of psychological intrigue.

These games not only provide cozy gameplay but also challenge your perception and creativity. As we continue to explore the gaming landscape, what other titles do you think will surprise us with their cozy yet thought-provoking mechanics?
