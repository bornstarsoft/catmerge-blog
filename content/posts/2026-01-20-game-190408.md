If youre someone who finds joy in tidying up and decorating, then the upcoming game Hozy might just be your next obsession. Combining elements from beloved titles like Unpacking and PowerWash Simulator, Hozy offers a unique sandbox experience where players can clean and decorate rooms to their heart's content. The freedom to arrange furniture and decor as you please makes it a perfect outlet for those who crave order and aesthetics in their gaming experience.

In Hozy, each level presents a new room to transform, starting with a blank canvas. Players can engage in satisfying cleaning mechanics, such as swirling garbage into floating balls before tossing them into a whimsical Tardis-like bin. Once the space is spotless, the real fun begins with an array of furniture options to personalize each room. The game encourages creativity and relaxation, allowing you to zone out to the ambient sounds while you create your ideal living space.
