Japanese indie developer Anettai has unveiled their upcoming title, Hakoniwa Electric, a delightful isometric puzzle game set to release on Windows PC in late 2026. This game promises a unique blend of relaxation and creativity, allowing players to explore charming stages while solving wiring puzzles to illuminate a miniature world. With no time limits or harsh penalties, its designed to be a soothing experience for players of all ages.

In Hakoniwa Electric, you step into the shoes of Akari, who takes over her grandfather's electronics shop while he enjoys a tropical vacation. The game features 12 intricately designed stages that reflect the changing seasons, each filled with adorable details and hidden puzzles. As you progress, Akaris Monthly Diary updates, providing a glimpse into her life and the townsfolk she interacts with.

This game stands out not just for its cute aesthetics but also for its gentle approach to puzzle-solving. It invites players to unwind and enjoy the process without the stress of difficult calculations or time constraints. As we look ahead to its release, one can't help but wonder how this cozy adventure will resonate with gamers seeking a peaceful escape in their gaming experiences.
