Nintendo Switch 2 owners can rejoice as one of the standout games of 2025, Blue Prince, has just launched on the platform. This surprise release adds to an already impressive lineup for the Switch 2, which has seen titles like Mario Kart World and Hades 2 in its early months. Blue Prince, developed by Dogubomb, is a captivating puzzle game with roguelike elements that invites players to unravel mysteries in an old manor, making it a must-have for fans of the genre.

With an outstanding Metacritic score of 92/100, Blue Prince has been hailed as not just the best puzzle game of the year, but one of the best games across all genres. It joins the ranks of legendary titles like Portal and Braid, solidifying its place in gaming history. For those eager to dive into this acclaimed experience, the game is currently available at a special launch price of $19.79 until March 12th.
