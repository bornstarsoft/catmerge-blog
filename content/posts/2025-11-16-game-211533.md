Get ready for an immersive experience as "Bus World" prepares to launch on Xbox Series X|S and PlayStation 5! This highly anticipated bus-driving simulator, developed by KishMish Games, offers players a unique opportunity to navigate through historically rich and diverse locations, including the haunting Chernobyl Exclusion Zone and the breathtaking landscapes of modern-day China and Iceland.

With its realistic driving mechanics and a variety of transport missions, players can engage in everything from standard routes to emergency rescue operations. The games free drive mode allows for exploration and creativity, making it a standout in the genre. As players progress, they can unlock a range of classic and modern bus models, enhancing their gameplay experience.

As we await the release on November 20 for Xbox and December 11 for PlayStation, the excitement is palpable. Will "Bus World" redefine bus simulation gaming? Only time will tell!
