Viewfinder stands out as a truly innovative puzzle game that challenges players to turn pictures into reality. Set against a mysterious backdrop, you engage in a first-person narrative where an unseen woman guides you through mind-bending puzzles. The core mechanic revolves around using found pictures to create pathways and environments, making each puzzle a unique challenge that requires both creativity and precision.

As you progress, the complexity of the puzzles increases, often leaving players stumped. Thankfully, the game includes a rewind feature, allowing you to correct misalignments and try again. The graphics, while initially simple, evolve as you unlock new areas, bringing vibrant colors and surprising elements into play. Coupled with solid voice acting, Viewfinder offers an engaging experience for puzzle enthusiasts.

For those seeking a fresh addition to the Nintendo Switch library, Viewfinder is a must-try. Its inventive gameplay and intriguing story will keep you hooked. Are you ready to take on the challenge and see how far your puzzle-solving skills can take you?
