Get ready for a vibrant gaming experience as ININ Games and TAITO announce the upcoming release of Parasol Superstars, a bundle featuring two beloved 2D platformers: Spica Adventure and Parasol Stars: The Story of Bubble Bobble III. Launching on April 14, 2026, for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4|5, and Xbox, this release marks the first-ever worldwide console debut of Spica Adventure, a title that has long been exclusive to Japan.

Players will embark on a colorful journey with Nico, navigating imaginative stages filled with quirky enemies and clever challenges. Armed with a versatile parasol, players must combine quick reflexes with strategic thinking to conquer each level. With 28 stages and branching paths, the replayability is immense, ensuring that every playthrough feels fresh and exciting.

As we look forward to this unique blend of nostalgia and new discovery, it raises the question: how will the gaming community embrace the return of these classic titles? The excitement is palpable, and the countdown to April 14 begins!
