The recently launched game AiliA by IceToad Studio LLC is captivating players with its innovative mirror-based gameplay mechanics. Now available on Steam for $11.99, AiliA invites players to step into a mesmerizing world where reality and reflection intertwine. With a 20% discount during its launch period, this game is not just a visual treat but also a brain-teasing experience that promises to engage puzzle enthusiasts.

In AiliA, players control a young girl named Aria as she navigates through beautifully crafted stages that blur the lines between the real world and a mirrored dimension. The interactive mirrors are not just for show; they serve as gateways to new paths and solutions, making every puzzle a unique challenge. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven distinct themed areas, players will find themselves immersed in a rich narrative that unfolds through the memories of sisters Aria and Lia.

For those eager to dive in, a free demo is available on Steam, allowing players to experience the enchanting gameplay firsthand. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, AiliA stands out as a testament to creativity in puzzle design. Will you take on the challenge and explore the depths of this reflective adventure?
