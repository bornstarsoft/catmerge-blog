In a world where productivity and discipline are often celebrated, it's refreshing to see someone like Zac Clark, a former Bachelorette star and addiction recovery advocate, embrace the simple pleasure of playing a puzzle game. Clark recently shared that his late-night guilty pleasure is none other than Blocky Blast, a free browser game that has him unwinding after long, structured days filled with work and marathon training.

Despite his rigorous daily routine, which includes early morning runs and meditation, Clark's candid admission about his late-night gaming habit resonates with many of us. It serves as a reminder that even the most disciplined individuals have moments where they seek relaxation and enjoyment, often in the form of a game that allows them to disconnect from the pressures of daily life.

As we navigate our own busy lives, perhaps we can take a cue from Clark's authenticity. It's okay to indulge in something that brings joy, even if it means swapping out meditation for a few rounds of clearing blocks. What are your guilty pleasures that help you unwind at the end of a long day?
