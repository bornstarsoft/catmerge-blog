Remedy Entertainment has officially unveiled Control Resonant, the eagerly awaited sequel to the acclaimed game Control, at The Video Game Awards. This action-adventure RPG promises to immerse players in a warped version of Manhattan, where they will navigate a reality-bending crisis while harnessing the extraordinary powers of Dylan Faden. The game is set to be released in 2026 on multiple platforms, including PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC.

In this sequel, players will not only face a mysterious cosmic entity that alters reality but will also explore a vast, unfamiliar world filled with perception-bending locations. Creative Director Mikael Kasurinen emphasizes that Control Resonant is designed to be accessible for newcomers while offering a deeper experience for returning fans. With an expanded combat system and a focus on player choice, this game aims to elevate the series to new heights.

As we look forward to its release, the anticipation builds around how Control Resonant will redefine the boundaries of storytelling and gameplay in the gaming universe. Are you ready to dive into this new chapter of the Remedy-verse?
