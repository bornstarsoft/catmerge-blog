In a bold move that could reshape the gaming landscape, ShiftUp has announced its acquisition of Unbound, a renowned Japanese game developer led by the legendary Shinji Mikami. Known for iconic titles like "Devil May Cry" and the "Biohazard" series, Unbound brings a wealth of experience and creativity to ShiftUp's portfolio, promising to enhance its PC and console game offerings significantly.

This acquisition not only allows ShiftUp to harness world-class talent but also positions the company to take charge of publishing new original IPs that Unbound is currently developing. With this strategic partnership, ShiftUp aims to strengthen its global presence and elevate the gaming experience for users worldwide. As CEO Kim Hyung-tae stated, this collaboration is set to solidify ShiftUp's competitive edge in the global market.
