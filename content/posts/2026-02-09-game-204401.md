Get ready to dive into a captivating dream world with the upcoming release of "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM" on February 10, 2026. This point-and-click puzzle game, developed by solo creator stone, invites players to rearrange objects like scraps of paper and leaves to solve intriguing puzzles. What sets this game apart is its unique puzzle system, where players can freely drag and drop objects to uncover hidden messages and solutions, making for a truly immersive experience.

As the third installment in the beloved HER TREES series, this game promises to deliver over 40 puzzles, significantly expanding on its predecessor. Even if you haven't played the previous titles, the game is designed to be enjoyed independently, allowing newcomers to jump right in. With a relaxing atmosphere and a non-verbal gameplay style, it's perfect for puzzle enthusiasts looking for a fresh challenge.
