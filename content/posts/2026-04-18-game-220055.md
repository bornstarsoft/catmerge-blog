PEARL ABYSS has made waves in the gaming industry with its open-world action-adventure title, 'Crimson Desert', achieving over 5 million copies sold globally in just 26 days. This remarkable feat not only sets a new record for the fastest sales pace in Korean console game history but also highlights the growing influence of Korean developers in the global gaming market.

The game's success is a testament to the dedication of the developers and the enthusiastic support from players, affectionately referred to as 'Greymanes'. This milestone surpasses even the acclaimed 'Clair Obscur: Expedition 33', which took five months to reach the same sales figure after winning Game of the Year at The Game Awards.

As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how 'Crimson Desert' continues to evolve and what new heights it can reach in the competitive gaming landscape. Will this success inspire more innovative titles from Korean developers in the future?
