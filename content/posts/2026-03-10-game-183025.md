If you're a fan of cozy puzzle games and cats, then "Kitty Haven" is a title you won't want to miss. This upcoming game invites players to create a perfect sanctuary for their feline friends, each with their unique personalities and needs. Whether its accommodating picky eaters or resolving cat squabbles, the game challenges you to arrange spaces creatively to restore harmony among your furry residents.

With the ability to unlock new items and residents, players can customize their havens while solving engaging puzzles. The demo is already available on Steam, allowing eager gamers to get a taste of the delightful challenges that await. As we look forward to its full release, one can't help but wonder: how will you design the ultimate retreat for your kittens?
