Bungie has officially announced the release date for its highly anticipated extraction shooter, Marathon, set to launch in March 2026. The game will be available on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC for a price of $39.99. This announcement comes after a delay that allowed the developers to refine the game based on community feedback, ensuring a more polished experience for players.

One of the most exciting aspects of Marathon is its commitment to a fair gaming environment. Bungie has confirmed that there will be no pay-to-win mechanics, and all gameplay updatesincluding maps and eventswill be accessible to players without additional costs. This approach not only enhances the gaming experience but also fosters a sense of community among players.

With new features like proximity chat and a solo experience mode, Marathon aims to engage players in a rich world filled with lore and loot. As we look forward to its release, it's clear that Bungie is dedicated to creating a game that resonates with its audience. Will Marathon set a new standard for extraction shooters in the gaming industry? Only time will tell.
