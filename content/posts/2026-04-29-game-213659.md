Get ready to embark on a whimsical adventure with "Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster," the latest mobile puzzle game from HAOPLAY, set to launch on iOS and Android later this year. Players will step into the webbed feet of Captain Quack, navigating a magical ark through a perilous ocean filled with monsters. This game promises not just a casual gaming experience but a strategic challenge as players select from a roster of up to fifty unique birds, each belonging to different classes with distinct abilities.

The gameplay revolves around a pachinko-style board where players fire eggs at adversaries, aiming to clear the board while racking up points. With the ability to evolve bird stats, alter abilities through a card system, and forge new equipment, players can tailor their strategies to tackle the game's challenges. Beyond the main campaign, additional modes like the Purgatory Tower and Starry Expedition offer varied gameplay experiences, ensuring that players can hone their skills or compete against others.

Pre-registration is now open, offering exciting rewards for early sign-ups. As we await the official launch, it's clear that "Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster" is shaping up to be a must-play title for puzzle game enthusiasts. Will you join Captain Quack on this epic journey?
