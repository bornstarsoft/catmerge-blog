Oh My Galaxy! is not just another action puzzle game; it's a delightful blend of creativity and challenge that captivates players of all ages. Released by nDreams for the Meta Quest, this game invites you to defend planets from invading asteroids by flinging your own projectiles in a visually stunning cosmic environment. The mechanics are simple yet engaging, making it accessible for both casual gamers and those seeking a more immersive experience.

What sets Oh My Galaxy! apart is its ability to seamlessly integrate mixed reality and virtual reality gameplay. Whether you're using controllers or hand-tracking, the controls are intuitive, allowing players to focus on the fun rather than the mechanics. The games charming aesthetic, combined with its engaging sound design, creates a captivating atmosphere that keeps players coming back for more.

With its straightforward yet rewarding gameplay, Oh My Galaxy! is perfect for quick gaming sessions or longer playtimes.
