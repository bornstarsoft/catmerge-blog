This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers are in for a treat with the addition of highly anticipated titles like **Assassins Creed Mirage** and **Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty** to the Game Catalog. These games are set to enhance the gaming experience for Extra and Premium tier members, making it an exciting month for PlayStation enthusiasts.

In addition to these blockbuster titles, players can also look forward to the newly released **Skate Story** and family-friendly options such as **Paw Patrol** games and **Lego Horizon Adventures**. Notably, the classic fighter **Soulcalibur III** joins the Premium Classic Catalog, appealing to nostalgic gamers eager to revisit this iconic title.
