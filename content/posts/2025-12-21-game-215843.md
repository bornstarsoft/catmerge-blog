SEGA is ramping up excitement for the half-anniversary of Persona5: The Phantom X with the upcoming release of update 2.7 on December 25. This update will introduce beloved characters Justine and Caroline from the original Persona 5, enriching the games roster and providing fans with a nostalgic connection to the series.

As a free-to-play gacha game, Persona5: The Phantom X offers players a unique twist on the Persona universe, featuring a new group of Phantom Thieves alongside familiar faces. The addition of Justine and Caroline not only enhances gameplay but also deepens the narrative ties to the original series, making this update a significant milestone for both new and veteran players.

In celebration of the half-anniversary, players can also look forward to daily log-in bonuses leading up to the update, including Meta Jewels and exclusive in-game items. With these exciting developments, its clear that Persona5: The Phantom X is committed to keeping its community engaged and entertained.
