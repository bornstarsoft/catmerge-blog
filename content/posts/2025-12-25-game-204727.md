Get ready for a fruity adventure as the beloved multiplayer party game, Fruit Mountain Party, is set to launch on Switch 2 and Switch in spring 2026! The game features exciting modes, including heated online battles for up to four players, where you can compete against friends or players worldwide. Whether you prefer a serious time attack or a chaotic battle filled with surprises, Fruit Mountain Party promises a delightful gaming experience. Plus, with a single-player mode that supports global rankings, players can hone their skills and aim for the top!
