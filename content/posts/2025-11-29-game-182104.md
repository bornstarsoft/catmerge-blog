Get ready for an enchanting adventure as ININ, in collaboration with Studio Siesta and Rocket Engine, prepares to launch Trouble Witches FINAL! Episode 01 Daughters of Amalgam this December. This delightful shooter brings back 12 adorable witches, each with unique abilities, in a visually stunning package that promises to captivate both casual gamers and hardcore bullet-hell enthusiasts alike.

The digital edition will be available on December 15 for Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 4, while collectors can snag the Physical Special Edition starting December 9. This special edition is packed with goodies, including an original soundtrack and a full-color artbook, making it a must-have for fans of the genre.

With its vibrant visuals and engaging gameplay mechanics, Trouble Witches FINAL! offers a fresh take on the Cute 'Em Up genre. The games unique combat system allows players to convert enemy bullets into gold coins, adding a strategic layer to the fast-paced action. As we look forward to its release, one can't help but wonder how this title will shape the future of indie shooters in the gaming landscape.
